International Organization for Migration is a UN agency in Kampala. Established in 1951, IOM is the leading inter-governmental organization in the field of migration and works closely with governmental, intergovernmental and non-governmental partners. With 155 member states, a further 11 states holding observer status and offices in over 100 countries, IOM is dedicated to promoting humane and orderly migration for the benefit of all. It does so by providing services and advice to governments and migrants.

At Uganda Hotel Owners Association
2008 – Aug 2013
Duration: 5 yrs 5 mos
Kampala, Uganda

I joined the Uganda Hotel Owners Association (UHOA) in April, 2008. This is the umbrella body that brings together all the hotels in Uganda. In Uganda, the hotel industry employs the largest number of the population and the most number of females in the economy second only to Agriculture. The UHOA offers the largest market for agricultural produce, the soft drink and alcohol industry and are some of the biggest investors and tax payers in Uganda's economy. My main duties included:

1. In charge of coordinating all the hotel activities in Uganda's tourism industry.
2. Organizing training workshops, seminars, meetings for member hotels on issues that affect the hotel industry.
3. Executive Secretary to the Board of the UHOA.
4. Board Representative to the Uganda General Managers Board.
5. I represent the Association at meetings with all stakeholders in the tourism industry. The stakeholders include the Ministry of Tourism, Federation of Uganda Employers, Uganda Tourism Board, Uganda Tourism Association, General Managers Monthly Meetings, Association of the Uganda Tour Operators and many others.
6.UHOA,Focal Person on HIV & AIDS to Federation of Uganda Employers (FUE).
7. Board Representative to Kyambogo University Curricullum Development Committee.
8. UHOA Representative on the Ministry of Tourism Hotel Grading and Classification Committee.
